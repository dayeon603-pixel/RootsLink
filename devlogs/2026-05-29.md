### Hypotheses
- H-reasons1: The barrier list in `score_reasons` is conservative — it only reports
  barriers that directly correspond to the hardcoded checks in the function body, not
  all possible combinations. This means users with complex barrier combinations may see
  incomplete explanations. A future improvement could generate a reason for every
  sub-score below a threshold.

- H-schema1: The choice to use `float` rather than `Annotated[float, Field(ge=0, le=1)]`
  for fields like `brain_drain_risk` in MatchResult means the service layer must
  validate the range. This is intentional — MatchResult is an output schema (read-only),

  would use country-level brain drain rates, but 0.5 avoids introducing bias before
  any survey data is collected.
